    ABOUT US

    Organisation’s history

    Founded in 2020, Bachpan emerged with a vision to create a world where every child’s dreams can flourish. Starting from providing them shelters, we have grown into a nurturing force for children’s well being.

    Mission

    Our mission is to empower children with the tools they need to thrive. We are committed to providing love, education, and support, ensuring every child has the opportunity to reach their full potential.

    Members

    Our team consists of passionate individuals, from child advocates to educators, all dedicated to creating a positive impact on children’s ives.

    Contribute

    Donation

    Support our mission by making a donation today. Your contribution directly impacts the lives of children, providing them with a chance for a brighter future.

    Volunteer

    Ready to make a difference in a child’s life? Explore our volunteer opportunities, sponsor a child, or support or fundraising events. Your involvement can bring joy to children in need.

    Spreading the word

    Join us in spreading the joy! Share our mission on social media, become a children’s advocate, host a “Dream Day” in your community and help create a ripple effect of kindness.
